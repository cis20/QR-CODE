Real-Life QR Code Scanning Incidents

In 2021, hackers used QR codes to steal personal data from unsuspecting victims in a series of attacks. One incident involved a scam where QR codes were placed on ATM machines. When scanned, they redirected victims to fake websites that collected their bank login details.

Another case occurred in a popular shopping mall where a malicious QR code redirected users to a page that downloaded malware onto their phones, allowing hackers to track personal information and access sensitive data.

These incidents highlight the risks of scanning unverified QR codes. Always verify the source of a QR code before scanning!
